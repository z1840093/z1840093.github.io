Yellow Lady's Slipper

Facts about the Yellow Lady's Slipper:

  • 4-24 inches tall
  • Perennial
  • Native plant
  • Grows in wet, chaded deciduous woods, swamps, and bogs
  • An orchid
  • Official flower of Door County
