My favorite hobby is horseback riding.

If you have never been horseback-riding in your life you should. Ever since I was a little girl my fanily always had horses growing up, so you can say horses are a part of my life, they are the most kind, sweet, gentle giants. My love for horseback-riding is more of a passion then a hobby. I enjoy it because there is no other feeling in the world that relaxes me as much as being on top of a horse, galloping in the summer and enjoy the breeze in my face while im doing something I love.


I always enjoy to teach people how to ride during the summer, it something that fascinates me and I love seeing peoples face, knowing they are having fun and enjoying it as much as I do. There are some downsides of it that some people just don't understand, people think bad about horses sometimes and that they are dangerous, horses are sweet and kind. As long as you respect the horse and are not afarid of it, the horse will respect you. Riding is something that I will always enjoy and will always teach people when I can, as long as I have the ability to I will continue to do so.
